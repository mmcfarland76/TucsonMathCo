About Me


  Hello! I'm Megan McFarland, the proud founder of Tucson Math Co. Originally hailing from the beautiful city of Seattle, I made the decision to follow my passion for mathematics and moved to the vibrant desert community of Tucson after completing my studies. I'm a devoted advocate for the world of numbers, and my journey in mathematics began long ago when I was just learning my times tables. Over the years, this passion has grown and evolved, ultimately leading me to pursue a degree in Applied Mathematics at UCLA. During my academic journey, I discovered a love for helping others understand and appreciate the beauty of math. This desire to share my passion led me to become a dedicated math tutor. I've been fortunate to work with students of all ages, from diverse backgrounds and abilities, helping them navigate the world of math with confidence and success.

Tucson Math Co. Journey

Tucson Math Co. was founded in 2016 with a vision to make mathematics accessible, engaging, and enjoyable for students of all backgrounds and abilities. My experiences and dedication led me to become a math tutor, where I've witnessed firsthand the transformative power of personalized learning. Driven by a belief in the potential of every student and the desire to make math a source of empowerment, Tucson Math Co. was born. Today, we continue to uphold my original vision, offering personalized tutoring that helps students conquer math challenges, build confidence, and develop a lifelong love for mathematics.
  At Tucson Math Co., I'm excited to bring affordable and accessible math tutoring to students in grades K-12. For just $20 an hour, your child can receive the extra math support they need to excel in their studies. Whether it's mastering the fundamentals or tackling advanced concepts, I'm here to guide them every step of the way.
