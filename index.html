More About Tucson Math Co.

At Tucson Math Co., we believe that every student has the potential to excel in mathematics. Founded by Megan McFarland, a passionate math enthusiast and experienced tutor, our mission is to make math accessible, enjoyable, and understandable for learners of all ages and abilities. With a strong commitment to personalized learning, we tailor our tutoring services to meet the unique needs of each student. Whether you're a kindergartener just starting your math adventure or a high school student preparing for advanced math exams, we have the expertise and dedication to help you succeed.

Our Approach

Our approach to math tutoring is simple yet effective:
  1. Personalized Learning: We understand that every student is different. That's why we start with an individual assessment to identify your strengths and areas that need improvement. This personalized approach ensures that your tutoring experience is tailored to your specific goals.
  2. Building Confidence: We believe that confidence is key to mastering math. Our tutors not only teach math concepts but also empower you to believe in your abilities. With the right guidance, you can overcome any math challenge.
  3. Affordable Rates: Quality math tutoring shouldn't break the bank. We offer our services at an affordable rate of just $20 an hour, making it accessible for students and families.

Our Services

  1. K-12 Math Tutoring: From basic arithmetic to advanced calculus, we cover the entire spectrum of K-12 math topics. Our tutors ensure that you have a strong foundation to excel in your math classes.
  2. Homework Help: Stuck on a tricky math problem? Our tutors are here to assist you with homework assignments, ensuring you understand the concepts.
  3. Test Prep: Are you preparing for standardized tests or college entrance exams? Our test prep programs help you master the math sections, increasing your chances of success.
